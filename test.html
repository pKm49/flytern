Cookies policy

Cookies are nothing but small text files stored by your browser or a particular/almost any website on your computer. This will help the website identify your device the next time you visit. This may be used to automatically log you in or remember your settings depending on the cookie placed by the website. The website cannot access any information on your computer other than the cookie it has set.

Cookies may be used to track your website browsing history. They track your activities or movements within the website such as remember your registered login, preferences, to resume where you left off and other customizations.

Cookies are not executable files. Hence are harmless. Cookies pose no risk since neither they contain virus in any form nor do they spy on your PC content to compromise security.

We use cookies in Og Travel to serve you better and personalize your experience with our website as well as advertisements. They are intended to help you access our website faster and more efficiently. Apart from improving user experience they also provide us with certain analytics which would help us understand how the website is being used and its effectiveness. This helps us improve/enhance our website to by adding more features.

Cookies may also be used by advertisements on our website. These are only used for tracking the effectiveness of advertisement placed on our website. Aggregate data of cookies may be shared with third parties for their promotional and marketing activities. No personal information will be collected via cookies.

Please note that if a user wishes to opt out of cookies, he/she can control cookies in our website by denying a cookie in its pop up window or block cookies in a browser. However, certain features to enhance user experience in our website are served by cookies. Hence removal of cookies may affect/degrade the performance and user experience of our website.
